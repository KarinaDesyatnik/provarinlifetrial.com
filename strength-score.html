Understanding Your Sexual Strength Score.
Now that you’ve calculated your score, it’s important to learn what that number means.
  • 55-60: Excellent sexual strength
    In this range, your 3 bio-drivers—testosterone, blood flow, and metabolism—are functioning at optimum levels for peak sexual performance. Because of this, your libido is healthy, your arousal quality is strong, and your sexual stamina is powerful. Your focus at this stage should be on maintaining your sexual strength by living healthfully and mindfully.
  • 50-54: Moderate sexual strength
    In the moderate range, the function of your 3 bio-drivers is beginning to weaken. Testosterone levels are starting to decrease, a gradual decline in nitric oxide production is impacting blood flow, and your metabolism may be starting to slow down. While your libido, arousal and stamina aren’t yet cause for concern your focus should be on curbing the diminishing of your sexual health through lifestyle changes such as eating nutrient dense foods, minimizing stress levels and exercising daily.
  • 49 or Below: Reduced sexual strength
    In this stage of reduced sexual strength, you are experiencing sexual decline. Low testosterone levels, significantly reduced blood flow due to diminished nitric oxide production, and a sluggish metabolism are key factors in your decreasing sexual strength. Low libido, difficulty achieving or maintaining an erection, and weakened sexual stamina are primary indicators of sexual decline.
